Data is carried out based on the data from August 25, 2013 to September 30, 2017.

I intended for the character who was higher than level 36,that is get from Losestone charactor search page, in this survey.And narrowed character that change hp,minion count,mount count from last survey to regard as active character.and exclude characters that has no mount as bot.
